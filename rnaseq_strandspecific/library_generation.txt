Generating a strand-specific RNAseq library using the illumina and
epicentre kits.

RNA Binding Plate
1.  Dilute total mRNA to 50 ul, 500 pg - 50 ng.  Thaw RNA beads.
2.  Vortex RNA beads
3.  Add 50 ulRNA purification beads; pipette 6x to mix

Incubate RNA Binding Plate
1.  Place on themocycler for mRNA denaturation cycle (65 C 5 min; 4 C)
2.  Remove when cycle reaches 4 C
3.  Place on bench 5 minutes to help binding

RNA Binding Plate Wash
1.  Place on magnetic stand 5 minutes to separate polyA
2.  Remove all supernatant
3.  Remove from stand
4.  Wash with 200 ul bead washing buffer, pipette mix 6x
5.  Place back on stand 5 minutes
6.  Remove/discard supernatant; do not disturb beads
7.  Remove plate from stand
8.  Add 50 ul elution buffer; mix 6x

Incubate RNA Binding Plate
1.  Place plate on thermocycler for mRNA elution (80 C 2 min; 25 C)
2.  Remove plate when it reaches 25 C

Make RNA Fragmentation Plate
1.  Add 50 ul bead binding buffer; mix 6x
2.  Incubate 5 min RT
3.  Put back on stand 5 min
4.  Remove/discard supernatant
5.  Remove from stand
6.  Wash with 200 ul bead washing buffer; mix 6x
7.  Back on stand 5 min
8.  Remove/discard supernatant
9.  Remove from stand
10. Add 19.5 ul of Elute, Prime, Fragment Mix to each sample; pipette
 mix 6x.  This contains random hexamers.

Incubate Fragmentation Plate
1.  Place on thermocycler (94 C 8 minutes; 4 C)
2.  Remove from thermocycler as soon as 4 C
3.  Place on magnetic stand RT 5 minutes
4.  Program thermocycler for first strand synthesis
    (25 C 5 minutes; 42 C 30 minutes; 37 pause)
5.  Place plate on stand 5 minutes
6.  Potentially transfer 12 ul to new tubes  

Epicentre cDNA Synthesis
1.  Make Master mix for each well containing
  3 ul cDNA synthesis mix, 0.5 ul DTT, 0.5 ul RT to a total 4 ul /
  sample
2.  Add 4 ul master mix to each sample
3.  Take to thermocycler pre-programmed for RT reaction
4.  Add 1 ul finishing enzyme to each sample at 37 C
5.  Incubate 37 C 10 min, 95 C 3 min, cool to 25 C

Epicentre Tag DNA
1.  Make tagging premix for each sample containing: 7.5 ul Tagging mix
and 0.5 ul DNA polymerase
2.  Add 8 ul mix to each sample
3.  Incubate RT 15 minutes followed by 95 C 3 minutes followed by 4 C

Epicentre cDNA purification
a.  Pre-make ~ 10 ml 80% ethanol
1.  Vortex beads until well dispersed.  Add 50 ul to each well, mix
10x
2.  Incubate RT 15 minutes
3.  Place on stand 5 minutes
4.  Remove 79 ul supernatant from each well 
5.  Add 200 ul 80% fresh EtOH
6.  Incubate 30 seconds RT, remove/discard supernatant
7.  Add 200 ul 80% fresh EtOH
8.  Incubate 30 seconds RT, remove/discard supernatant
9.  Let stand RT 15 minutes to dry
10. Add 20 ul water and remove from magnetic stand
11. Mix to fully resuspend beads
12. Incubate plate 2 minutes RT
13. Place plate on stand 5 minutes
14. Transfer cDNA to fresh plate

Epicentre PCR amplification
1.  For each tube containing ~ 22.5 ul cDNA add
  a) 25 ul FailSafe PCR enzyme mix
  b) 1 ul Forward PCR primer
  c) 1 ul Reverse PCR primer
  d) 0.5 ul FailSafe PCR enzyme (1.25)
2.  PCR denature 1 min 95
  a) 12-15 cycles of 95 C 30 seconds
  b) 55 C 30 seconds
  c) 68 C 3 minutes

  d) final extension 7 minute

Epicentre Final Cleaning
a.  Pre-make ~ 10 ml 80% ethanol
1.  Vortex beads until well dispersed.  Add 50 ul to each well, mix
10x
2.  Incubate RT 15 minutes
3.  Place on stand 5 minutes
4.  Remove 79 ul supernatant from each well 
5.  Add 200 ul 80% fresh EtOH
6.  Incubate 30 seconds RT, remove/discard supernatant
7.  Add 200 ul 80% fresh EtOH
8.  Incubate 30 seconds RT, remove/discard supernatant
9.  Let stand RT 15 minutes to dry
10. Add 20 ul water and remove from magnetic stand
11. Mix to fully resuspend beads
12. Incubate plate 2 minutes RT
13. Place plate on stand 5 minutes
14. Transfer cDNA to fresh plate
